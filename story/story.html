SIMMONS HISTORY

Founded in 1870, Simmons has built a legacy of comfort, craftsmanship, and innovation.
From the United States to Korea, Simmons continues to define premium sleep.

1870-1876

From Foundation to Innovation

Beginning in Kenosha, Wisconsin, Zalmon G. Simmons laid the foundation for the brand, and soon transformed mattress production through a patented spring manufacturing machine, making innovation, high-quality mattresses, and wider accessibility possible.

1925-1931

Innovation Shaping the Future of Sleep

With the world's first patented pocket spring manufacturing machine, Simmons introduced Beautyrest and continued pioneering sleep research, helping lay the foundation for modern sleep science.

1958-1970

Innovation That Redefined Rest

Recognizing changes in the average American body size, Simmons introduced the world's first queen- and king-size mattresses, setting a new standard for sleep. Through distinctive and innovative advertising campaigns, Simmons delivered its brand spirit with strong impact.

2017-2018

ESG Leadership in Mattress Safety

As part of its ESG management, Simmons Korea became the first and only company in Korea to test all household mattresses against international and Korean fire safety standards, while developing patented flame-resistant mattresses.

2024

For the Public Good

Guided by its commitment to the public good, Simmons Korea disclosed its patented flame-resistant mattress manufacturing technology to support broader safety across the bedding industry.

2025

100 Years of Beautyrest

Marking the 100th anniversary of Beautyrest, Simmons became the first in Korea to apply vanadium to POSCO steel wire, advancing its legacy of undisturbed comfort through a new innovation.

TECHNOLOGY

Pocket Spring

Simmons signature pocket spring system delivers optimal elasticity, stability, and durability, providing even support that closely adapts to the body.

Layering

By layering more than 50 carefully selected cushioning materials over pocket springs, Simmons creates a uniquely refined sleep experience with optimal comfort.

ZONING SYSTEM

Simmons’ proprietary zoning system combines pocket springs with varying levels of support and elasticity to adapt to different body shapes, sleep habits, and lifestyles.

Vanadium

Vanadium, also used in the aerospace industry, provides exceptional strength, flexibility, and durability, performing reliably even under extreme pressure and heat.
